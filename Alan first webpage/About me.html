Welcome To Alan's Website

This page will showcase about me and my family

On weekends, our family occasionally goes to Dongqian Lake for sightseeing. There are many interesting places near Dongqian Lake, such as Old Street, Ten Mile Four Villages, etc. Sometimes we also have barbecues and row boats by the creek

I have many hobbies. I had studied skateboarding for a while before. I liked it very much at the time, but then I got injured when I slipped down from a very high place, and then I stopped skateboarding. I often play badminton with my classmates, so that I can exercise my body effectively and there is no danger like skateboarding. I also love listening to music, and I can forget all the things that bother me and enjoy music to the fullest.

I used to work part-time at Pizza Hut for a while. I learned many things in just half a year, such as setting plates, making drinks and pizza, and so on. These part-time hours allowed me to meet a lot of friends, both in college and in high school. I also gained more experience in life and learned a lot of cleaning skills.
